My Favorite Animal: Dragon

What is a dragon?

Dragons are mythological creatures. They are prevalent in cultures across the ancient world from Northern Europe to Japan. These animals come in all types of shapes and sizes, with personalities and temperments as varied as their appearances.


Characteristics of Dragons

  • Winged
  • Breath fire, and sometimes ice, electricity or poison gas
  • Can be highly intelligent and kind
  • Some are greedy and reclusive
  • Others are devious plotters that meddle in human affairs
  • Dragons can have a human form
  • Some dragons are wild and animalistic
